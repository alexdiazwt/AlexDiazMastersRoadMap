# Alex Diaz Masters Roadmap

Welcome! This roadmap is designed to guide students and me through their academic journey, whether pursuing a Bachelor's, Master's, or similar degree. It provides a structured approach to planning courses, research, and milestones.

## Purpose

- Help students organize their studies and track progress.
- Offer a template adaptable for various academic programs.
- Share resources, tips, and best practices.

## How to Use

1. **Clone or download** this repository.
2. **Customize** the roadmap for your degree requirements.
3. **Update** milestones and tasks as you progress.

## Sections

- Coursework planning
- Research/project tracking
- Key deadlines and milestones
- Useful resources
